Mangyongdae fufair

Mangyongdae funfair is an attraction in pyongyang and opened april 1982 It is still open and operational but it has low repairs and looks rundown. The funfair can be packed or deserted because the location is at the edge of the city. Mangyondgae funfair features a roller coaster and a merry go round. The park received some criticism from Kim-Jong-Un in 2012 which made the park close for a moment so they could repair the attraction.

Ryugyong Hotel

Ryugyong hotel holds the world record for the tallest unoccupied building. The building is a 105 floor hotel which is deserted. In 1987 the construction started on Ryugyong Hotel in pyongyang. The hotel was going to be over 1000 feet tall, have a nightclub, a bowling alley, five restaurants and have over 3000 rooms. The construction halted in 1992 because of the economical crisis North Korea had when the soviet union collapsed. In 2008 the construction continued but got canceled year 2012
