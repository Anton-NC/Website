Sights in Pyongyang

In North Korea there is always something to do, with exotic places with different kinds of foods such as traditional or street food. The first sight in Pyongyang you can see is the two supreme leaders represented with statues which you should always treat with respect and never show your back towards their direction. The Juchetower is an monument in pyongyang. The tower is located on the west bank of the flood Taedong, and was built in 1982 which was to honor Kim Il Sung's 70th anniversary. Its stands at 170 meter high and has a total blocks of 550 and only the spiral on top weighs and astonishing 45 tons. Munsu Water park is also an good place for you with a broad range of activities.
